Reviews Summary

McCarthy Honors Residential College is known for its new and clean facilities, with room common spaces being cleaned twice a week. The dorm offers a variety of setups in study lounges on every floor, perfect for studying alone or with friends. Residents appreciate the great amenities such as AC, individual bathrooms, microwaves, and fridges. The location in the Village is convenient, close to cafes and shops. While some found the dorm less social due to its suite-style setup and closed door policy, others praised the spacious rooms, en-suite bathrooms, and the overall cleanliness of the building. Some students mentioned that the dorm culture could be a bit cliquey, but overall, McCarthy Honors Residential College is highly recommended for its prime location, great amenities, and a pleasant living environment that feels like a hotel experience.
