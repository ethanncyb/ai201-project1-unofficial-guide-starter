# Guide: Off-Campus Housing Options

Are you in need to research off-campus housing options? Here are some suggestions..

## University Park
Located close to USC campus, these apartment buildings or rental homes are generally located close to campus on the north, east, or west sides. It is recommended to live within the DPS patrol area and the north side of campus is most popular.

- University Gateway: https://www.livegw.com/university-gateway-los-angeles-ca
- Icon Plaza: https://iconplazausc.com/
- West 27th: https://west27thplace.com/
- Lorenzo: https://www.thelorenzo.com/
- Tuscany: https://www.tuscanyonfig.com/
- City Park: https://www.cityparkusc.com/
- Alumni Management Properties: http://alumnimgt.net/index.php
- Horizon Properties: http://www.trojanbuildings.com/
- Terra Property Group: https://www.terrapropertygroup.com/
- CDI Properties: http://www.uscspots.com/
- First Choice Housing: https://firstchoicehousing.com/
- Nova Student Housing: https://www.novastudenthousing.com
- Stuho Student Housing: http://stuho.com/
- Menlo Apartments: https://www.menlousc.net/
- Ellendale Arms: https://www.ellendalearms.com/
- Mosaic Properties: https://livewithmosaic.com/
- Community Builders Management: https://livecbm.com/

## Downtown Los Angeles (DTLA)
These apartment communities are located in downtown Los Angeles and popular with USC students looking to move away from campus and enjoy downtown living. It is generally less than a 10 minute drive or quick commute to campus by Expo line (light rail) from any of these properties.

- Medici: https://www.themedici.com/
- Pegasus: https://www.equityapartments.com/los-angeles/financial-district/pegasus-apartments
- Orsini: https://www.theorsini.com/
- CityLights: http://www.citylightsonfig.com/
- Met Lofts: https://www.berkshirecommunities.com/apartments/ca/los-angeles/met-lofts/
- Onyx: https://www.onyxdtla.com/
